$$ K = 3i+j \implies j = K-3j $$
$$ P = iA + jB $$

$$ K = \frac{P+3iB-iA}{B} $$


$$ K = \frac{P_x+3iB_x-iA_x}{B_x} = \frac{P_y+3iB_y-iA_y}{B_y} $$
$$ \frac{P_x}{B_x}+\frac{i(3B_x-A_x)}{B_x} = \frac{P_y}{B_y}+\frac{i(3B_y-A_y)}{B_y} $$
